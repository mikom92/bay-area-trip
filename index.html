Confirmed by the team Menlo Park hotel is covered Sun 20 – Thu 24 (5 nights, that bootcamp week) — not just Tue–Thu. Check-in Sunday frees up Monday as a bonus day.
Reserve early Alcatraz (90-day window) and Muir Woods parking both sell out — lock these in first.
